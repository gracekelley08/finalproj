My Favorite Activity


My favorite hobby is to play bass with my band, Anticline. We are a Harrisonburg, VA based DIY indie/pop/punk band. Anticline started in January 2024 and has 5 members. We write our own songs and currently have 8 originals that we play. Our song, “I Like Him!” will be featured in the SMAD short film, “Eclipse” on May 7th at SMADfest. We love performing and have played at Crayola, JMU GIVE’s Lei-down, and Kappa Pi’s Art-fest.

I started playing bass in May 2022 and joined Project Serenity, the first band I ever played with, that June. We played many gigs in Virginia and even JMU’s 2023 Purple Reign! We were lucky enough to be featured in JMU’s newspaper, The Breeze, as well.

Playing with a band has given me many amazing opportunities and improved my skills as a musician and a performer. My two basses and amp are my prized possessions!

The First Bass I Tried


The first bass I ever played was a Squire J Affinity Bass Guitar in blue. My friend let me use it over the summer to see if I enjoyed playing. This 4-string is an affordable beginner level bass. It is made from poplar and maple wood, has two single-coil Jazz Bass pickups, and roundwound strings. This bass was relatively reliable. It was played for many years and had no issues until a small electrical problem when I used it.

The First Bass I Owned


During 2022 summer I got my first bass for my birthday! It is a Sterling by Music Man StingRay in Olive. I went to my local Guitar Center and tested lots of basses out and this was my favorite within my budget. It is a step above a beginner bass and is good quality....
